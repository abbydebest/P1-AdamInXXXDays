Walk around

Start your third and final day at the AUAS campus to explore the area around AMFI. Walk along the main road in between the tall buildings. Don't be shy to enter any HVA building.

AUAS
campus

AUAS has multiple buildings. The newest addition, Jacoba mulder.

Fest
lunch

Next visit the student 'pub' on the AUAS campus. Here you can have a taste of the student culture in the Netherlands and of the Dutch snacks. Make sure you try 'bitterballen'.

Fest
lunch

Fest, the place where school ends and the week-end begins. The place to be.

Oosterpark

Time for some nature. Walk in east direction for just about 8 minutes. Here you find 'het oosterpark'. A great place to wind down, take a stroll or to even study in the sun. Recharge here before our next spot.

Het Oosterpark

The first big park initiated by the mayors office, in 1891.

Cycle around

Now that you have recharged, rent a bike at oosterpark and cycle in the direction of the city center. Stay on the (red) cycling paths and hold your wheel diagonal on the tracks.

Cycle
around

The oosterpark bike rental shop. Ask for a bike that fits your cycling abilities.

Stedelijk museum

When you enter the city center go in the direction of 'museumplein'. Park and leave your bike at the bike rental shop 'museumplein'. End your route with a visit to 'stedelijk museum'.

Stedelijk
museum

A museum for modern and contemporary art, since 1874.
